Named Batea Scholars have the ability to take credit for participating in Batea research (although this is not required).

Specfically, Scholars:

  • Will be mentioned in data and research credits ( unless you specifically request not to be by contacting the researchers)
  • Will be welcome and encouraged to promote your participation in the Batea project
  • Are allowed to interact with researchers to improve the project
  • Will be able to download and interact with their own browsing pattern data

    • Learn more about the benefits of participating in Batea research

It is possible that you will find the infrequent survey popups annoying, or distracting.

There is no obligation for you to fill out any given survey, and you can turn them off entirely in the extension options page.

Learn more about how to turn off the surveys.

There is always a small chance that your information could be accidentally released to the Internet.

If you are a clinician, researcher, or other kind of expert, your specific clinical browsing patterns could potentially be especially damaging to you. In the worst case, this could lead to accusations of imcompetence or malpractice.

Some academic researchers continue to regard participation with Wikipedia as unsound. It might be possible that some academics could regard participation as the pre-publication of academic results.

Learn more about special risks to clinical, academic, or research professionals.
