What's Involved

Browsing patterns included in the study are securely uploaded to the Batea servers.

Other browsing patterns are ignored but forgotten.

Browsing patterns included in the study:

  • Browsing history of clinical concepts
  • Browsing history related to clinical Wikipedia sites
  • Optionally browsing history related to any Wikipedia sites

Read a complete description of which browser patterns are uploaded. (opens in a new tab)

What's Involved

Once you have installed the browser app, the Batea icon will appear in your Chrome search bar.

The icon will be colored black and white when Batea is not recording browser history.

What's Involved

The Batea icon will turn green to indicate that your current browsing session will be uploaded.

What's Involved

If you do not want your current browsing session to be uploaded, click the green Batea icon and turn the toggle to the "off" position.

This will turn the icon red, which means it is not recording.
